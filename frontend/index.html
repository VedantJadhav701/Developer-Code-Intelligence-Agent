FAQ

Is DevAgent really 100% local? +
Yes. DevAgent uses Ollama to run models on your machine. No code ever leaves your project root.
What models do you recommend? +
We recommend qwen2.5-coder:3b for its perfect balance of speed and patching accuracy on standard hardware.
Does it support JavaScript? +
Currently, the Environment Runtime is optimized for Python. Multi-language support is on the roadmap for v4.0.
